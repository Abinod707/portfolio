My Process

How I work

Every great design starts with understanding — the user, the business, and the context.

01
Discover
Week 1–2
User InterviewsSurveysCompetitive Analysis

Before touching any design tool, I immerse myself in the problem — talking to real users, studying the competition, and uncovering insights that most designers skip.

  • Stakeholder & user interviews
  • Competitor analysis and gap identification
  • Research synthesis and insight documentation
02
Define
Week 2–3
User PersonasJourney MapsProblem Statements

Synthesizing findings into clear problem statements and design principles that align everyone around what we're solving and why.

  • User personas grounded in real research
  • Journey maps to identify friction points
  • Clear, testable problem statements and success metrics
03
Ideate
Week 3–4
SketchingCrazy 8sMoodboarding

Creativity runs free. Dozens of concepts sketched, visual directions explored through moodboards, pushing beyond the obvious.

  • Rapid sketching to explore ideas quickly
  • Moodboards for visual tone and direction
  • Information architecture and user flow mapping
04
Design
Week 4–7
FigmaAdobe XDPhotoshop

From wireframes to polished high-fidelity screens — obsessive attention to detail. Every spacing, colour, and interaction is intentional.

  • Low-fidelity wireframes to validate structure first
  • High-fidelity mockups with pixel-perfect execution
  • Interactive prototypes in Figma for realistic testing
05
Test
Week 7–8
Usability TestingA/B TestingFeedback Loops

Designs tested with real users. Insights feed directly into iterations ensuring the final product genuinely solves the problem.

  • Moderated usability sessions with target users
  • Feedback synthesis and prioritised backlog
  • Multiple rounds until key tasks succeed
06
Deliver
Week 8–9
Handoff SpecsAsset ExportDev Support

Smooth handoff with comprehensive specs, assets, and documentation. I stay involved through development to ensure the vision is faithfully realised.

  • Pixel-perfect design specs with annotations
  • Organised asset libraries (PNG, SVG, PDF)
  • Developer Q&A support through implementation
